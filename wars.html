Event War

Event War is built into Towny.jar. It is begun using "/ta toggle war" and presently is restricted to nation vs nation wars and requires an admin or commandblock to activate.

Event War does not persist between reloads/server stops. Event war is very versatile as far as configuration and allows for completely non-destructive wars for money, up to wars involving complete territorial control and money.

Event War will be recieving a significant update expanding its possibilities towards player-initiated war, war perisistance across server restarts, and wars involving non-nation-vs-nation entities.

Flag War

Flag War is built into Towny.jar. When the config "allow_attacks" is true all enemy-nations will be able to start nation vs nation combat with territory control enabled.

Flag War is 24/7 and can be very punishing in regards to towns being able to suffer tremendous loss of territorial control.

Flag War is transitioning towards becoming a separate plugin run along-side Towny.jar.

Siege War

Siege War is available as a separate plugin, allowing for player-enabled, multi-day long Sieges.

Sieges are conducted by Nations on Towns resulting in a geo-political battle for influential territorial control. Sieges are non-destructive of town lands and only come with monetary punishments, negated with the Towny Bankruptcy system.

Siege War also supports 'casual' and 'cross-timezone' player bases. Each siege is typically of a moderate duration (e.g. 3 days), which has the advantage of allowing both 'dedicated' and 'casual/cross-timezone' players to become involved and contribute meaningfully to most wars.

3rd party war plugins

TownyWars

A simple plugin that provides arcade wars with your Towny plugin. The author of the plugin is karlov_m. TownyWars is a fairly new plugin, but it is already able to provide simple, and most importantly interesting wars between towns.

How it works
There are neutral towns and non-neutral ones. Only non-neutral cities can declare war. After the Declaration of war, both cities have points that are calculated from the number of citizens. When a citizen from one city is killed by a citizen from another, one point is taken away from the town of the victim and transferred to the attacking city. During the war, both towns cannot turn off the PvP. The winner gets the entire territory of the losing town. Other towns can join the war as allies of one of the parties.

BetterTownyWars

BetterTownyWars provides new mechanics for letting your players to fight between their nations with rewards for the winner, allowing them to be neutral too.​

Wars can be declared by a player if he/she has permissions. The plugin actually supports two types of war, and the winner earns a certain amount of money from the other nation bank.

War types
  • Nation vs Nation: Two nations against each other and the first one to kill a certain % of the residents of the enemy nation wins.
  • Town vs Town: Two towns fight and the first one to kill a certain % of the residents of the enemy nation wins.
A war can be ended if all of their members send a peace request or when it reaches the maximum duration time (disabled at the moment).
